CORE FEATURES

Four Intelligent Modules

From final approach to taxi operations — intelligent support at every stage of flight.

MODULE 01

Approach Stability Monitoring

Analyzes runway centerline alignment and descent stability using cockpit camera imagery and GPS/altitude data. Provides visual and voice alerts when speed, descent rate, or glide path deviate from safe ranges.

Unstable Approach Warning Glide Path Monitoring Energy State Prediction Runway Alignment
MODULE 02

Energy-Based Landing Assistance

Predicts landing feasibility zones and calculates runway reachability in real time based on aircraft speed and altitude. Detects excessive speed or insufficient energy conditions early to support go-around decisions.

Landing Zone Prediction Go-Around Decision Support Real-time Reachability
MODULE 03

Ground Collision Prevention

Detects nearby aircraft, vehicles, and obstacles during taxi using camera feeds and airport map data. Immediately alerts pilots of runway crossing conflicts and potential close-collision situations.

Runway Incursion Alert Taxiway Guidance Surface Traffic Monitor Blind Spot Detection
MODULE 04

Enhanced Situational Awareness

Overlays runway centerlines, taxiways, and aircraft position data onto camera imagery even in low-visibility or night conditions. Pilots can quickly identify hazards through an intuitive heads-up interface.

AR Overlay Night Vision Support Low Visibility Ops
