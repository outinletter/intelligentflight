MODULE 05 — ADS-B INTELLIGENCE

Flight Pattern Anomaly Detection

Airport-specific ADS-B trajectory databases power real-time deviation detection — going far beyond fixed thresholds.

Data-driven risk,
not just fixed limits.

Traditional warning systems trigger on absolute values. AIFAS learns from thousands of real approach and taxi trajectories at each airport, building statistical baselines for position, speed, altitude, descent rate, turn patterns, and runway entry timing.

When the current aircraft deviates from that learned envelope, pilots receive early, contextual alerts — calibrated to the actual operating environment of that specific airport.

KEY ANALYSIS PARAMETERS

Approach Speed Deviation

Flags speeds statistically outside the normal envelope for the specific runway and procedure.

Centerline Deviation

Compares lateral position against historical alignment data for the same approach.

Abnormal Descent Rate

Detects descent rates that deviate significantly from typical glide profiles at that airport.

Altitude-to-Speed Energy

Analyzes the combined energy state relative to the expected profile at each distance-to-threshold.

Turn & Approach Angle

Identifies abnormal turning patterns and non-standard intercept angles during approach.

Ground Movement Collision Risk

Predicts collision probability during taxi based on historical surface movement patterns.

AI-BASED RISK PREDICTION
